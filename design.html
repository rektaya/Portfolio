Angry Kitchen Hood

Kitchen hood is a a crucial part of the kitchen: it removes smoke, odours, even suppresses fire. Nevertheless, people don't appreciate it. They never clean it, always complain how old and dirty it is. Time passes, and kitchen hood becomes more and more angry about how it's being treated. That's how your ordrinary kitchen hood can turn out to be an angry kitchen hood.
